Background Panel

This panel allows the removal of the background. The average of a number of pixels from each end of the trace is taken and subtracted from the axis in question. For example, in constant delay subtraction, 5 pixels from the end of each column are averaged and the average is subtracted from the entire column.

The Background Panel: No subtraction

No change has been made.

Delay/Frequency Points
The number of points from each side of the trace to average for subtraction.
Subtract Const. Delay/Freq.
Subtract the average from the appropriate dimension.
Remove Negatives
Removes the negative values created in the background subtraction.

The Background Panel: Frequency subtraction

The frequency background has also been removed.

Note:

Binner does not discard the negative values in the trace until the "Binning" step. It does not display them, but they are still there. Therefore, subtracting multiple times will have no effect (with out changing any parameters). To get around this (and to conform to the commercial code) use the "Remove Negatives" button. It does just what it says and discards any negative values.
